Last year, I interned at the Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Laboratory. My team won first in the intern challenge in competition with other intern teams in our sector with a payload design. This payload would be mounted to the BlueRobotics BlueBoat and was able to monitor and remove algae from an on-campus testing pond.
